What we think

We love music, and your music matters. How big is your collection, how many tracks you can send per month should not be an issue when aiming to distribute it worldwide. While giant companies are building fast and sustainable network to distribute the music they choose to enlight, too many small, or even medium-sized labels and artists are left on the sidelines.

We want to change that.

This is why we create tools, and offer services. We believe in Free Software, because it serves you more. We build distributed networks, because they are more efficient.

Get onboard with us, and together we can build the distribution network of tomorrow.
